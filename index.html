Does a Satellite Solve Your Problem?

A practitioner's framework for assessing whether Earth observation data addresses a social monitoring need — and whether the investment is proportionate.

Working paper, 2025 · Fermi estimates are illustrative — label yours accordingly

The Five Steps
Key insight
Don't ask "what data do you need?" Ask "what decision are you making, and when?" People can't specify satellite parameters — but they can describe the choice in front of them. Work backwards from the decision to the data requirement.
Four Possible Outcomes
The framework doesn't always recommend a new satellite. These are the four typed outputs the gap analysis can produce.
Public vs. Paid Data
Gap analysis should be conducted first against publicly accessible data (Landsat, Sentinel, MODIS, PACE). If the gap can be closed with free data plus better intermediaries, that is the answer. Recommending paid commercial data (Planet Labs, Maxar) without flagging access equity implications is an error.
